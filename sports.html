The “World’s Fastest Swimmer” Sets Record – In Zero Gravity

In an astonishing display of athleticism, Olympic swimmer Michael Phelps has set a new world record for the fastest swim — in zero gravity. Using a specially designed swimming pool aboard the International Space Station (ISS), Phelps managed to complete a 100-meter freestyle in just 36.5 seconds, breaking his own Earth-bound record.
